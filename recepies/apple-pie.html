Apple Pie

About Apple Pie

This is a very simple tasty recepe for apple pie. While some people like theirs with cinnamon or other spices added in, I like mine plain. Apples all the way!

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 110g plain flour
  • 50g cooking margarine
  • Pinch of salt

Filling:

  • Cooking apples
  • Sugar (to taste)

Steps

For the crust:

  1. Crumble the margarine into the flour and salt.
  2. Add a small splash of water, to bind all together.
  3. Let the pastry rest for half an hour in the fridge.

For the filling:

  1. Peel and chop the apples into a deep pan.
  2. Add sugar to taste.
  3. Put the lid on the pan, light the stove and keep the mix at a slow simmer.
  4. Cook until the apples are falling apart and there is a nice sauce.
  5. Let the mix cool down before putting the pie together.

Putting it all together:

  1. Roll out almost two thirds of the pastry to the size of the tin.
  2. Chop off the edges of the pastry, leaving a little space to join the lid.
  3. Add the apple sauce.
  4. Roll out the rest of the pastry to make the lid. If you are artistic, make patterns!
  5. Put the lid over the rest of the pie and crimp the edges of the pastry together.
  6. If there are no holes in the pastry, cut two small slits in to the top of the pastry, to let the steam out.
  7. Cook in the oven at a medium temperature, til the pastry is light brown and crunchy.

And there you have it! One lovely simple apple pie to share with friends and family.
