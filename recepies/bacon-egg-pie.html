Bacon and Egg Pie

About Bacon and Egg Pie

Going on a picnic or a house party? Bacon and egg pie is a classic dish for these occasions. With just a few ingredients, you can make a nice, filling meal, that's very portable.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 110g plain flour
  • 50g cooking margarine
  • Pinch of salt

Filling:

  • Strips of bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • Half an onion

Steps

For the crust:

  1. Crumble the margarine into the flour and salt.
  2. Add a small splash of water, to bind all together.
  3. Let the pastry rest for half an hour in the fridge.

For the filling:

  1. Chop up the bacon and dice the onion.
  2. Fry the bacon and onion in a pan.
  3. Whisk up the eggs.
  4. Mix the eggs, bacon and onion, and add a pinch of salt.

Putting it all together:

  1. Roll out the pastry to the size of the tin.
  2. Chop off the edges of the pastry, leaving enough around the edges of the tin for shrinkage.
  3. Prick the bottom of the pastry.
  4. Add the filling.
  5. Put in the oven at around 200C and bake until the crust is crumbly and the egg mix is cooked through.

Now go and enjoy your picnic!
